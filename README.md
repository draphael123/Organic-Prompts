## Philosophy

**The goal is to scaffold thinking, not complete the work.**

Cursor's AI can help team members:
- Break down complex assignments into manageable steps
- Identify key concepts from videos/articles
- Structure their thinking and organize ideas
- Brainstorm and explore possibilities
- Get unstuck when they hit a wall

Cursor's AI should NOT:
- Write the entire response for them
- Generate the final polished answer
- Replace their own analysis and creativity

## How to Use This System

1. **When you receive a mini-workshop assignment:**
   - Open Cursor in this workspace
   - Use the appropriate prompt template for brainstorming
   - Let AI help you think through concepts and structure ideas
   - Write your final response in your own words

2. **Available Resources:**
   - `prompt-templates/` - Reusable prompt templates for different workshop types
   - `examples/` - Example conversations showing how to use prompts effectively
   - `guides/` - Step-by-step guides for common workshop formats

3. **Best Practices:**
   - Start with a prompt to explore concepts
   - Use follow-up prompts to dive deeper into specific areas
   - Always synthesize and rewrite in your own words
   - Ask for clarification, not completion

## Workshop Types

- **Video Analysis Workshops** - Breaking down educational videos and extracting concepts
- **Experiment Design** - Creating testable ideas based on new learnings
- **Case Study Analysis** - Analyzing examples and drawing insights
- **Strategy Development** - Building actionable plans from frameworks
